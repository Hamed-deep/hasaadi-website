٢. خطط الاشتراك

⚠️ غير قابلة للاسترداد بعد التفعيل، إلا في حالات استثنائية:

  • الاشتراك غير مُفعَّل: استرداد كامل خلال 24 ساعة من الدفع

  • إلغاء قبل انتهاء المدة: لا يُسترد الرصيد المتبقي

٣. الإعلانات المميزة

  • إعلان لم يُعرض بسبب خطأ تقني: استرداد كامل
  • إعلان مرفوض لمخالفة الشروط: غير قابل للاسترداد
  • إعلان قيد المراجعة أكثر من 48 ساعة: استرداد كامل

🌿 Our Commitment

We always strive to ensure customer satisfaction and support food security. If you are not satisfied with your experience, our team is here to assist you through the platform's secure internal financial intermediation system.

Scope: This policy applies to transactions within the Sultanate of Oman only. Electronic payments and refunds are made in Omani Rial (OMR) via the secure in-app payment gateway. Cash or off-app transactions are at both parties' own risk.

1. Physical Agricultural & Animal Products

Situation Action Timeframe / Conditions
Visibly damaged or spoiled product Full refund & order cancellation At inspection, before handing OTP to agent
Product different from description Full refund At inspection, before handing OTP to agent
Hidden defect (sealed seeds, supplements, etc.) Full refund or replacement Within 24 hours of receipt (subject to admin review)
Non-delivery Full refund After confirmed delivery deadline exceeded
Delivery delayed more than 48 hours Cancellation & full refund or compensation After exceeding 48 hours from specified time
Buyer changed mind after receipt Not eligible for refund —
⚠️ Critical Note

The buyer handing over the digital verification code (OTP) to the delivery agent constitutes an official acknowledgment of inspecting the goods and confirming their integrity. The platform's system will immediately release the held amount to the seller's account.

2. Subscription Plans

⚠️ Non-refundable after activation, except in exceptional cases:

  • Subscription not yet activated: Full refund within 24 hours of payment
  • Technical error by Hasaadi: Full refund after verification
  • Activated and used subscription: Non-refundable
  • Cancellation before expiry: Remaining balance is non-refundable

3. Featured Advertisements

  • Ad not displayed due to technical error: Full refund
  • Ad rejected for violating terms: Non-refundable
  • Ad under review for more than 48 hours: Full refund upon request
  • Approved and active ad: Non-refundable

4. How to Submit a Refund Request

  • 1. Contact: support@hamedapps.com or WhatsApp +968 9526 8707
  • 2. Details: Order number + seller name + reason for refund
  • 3. Evidence: Photos or video of the product (before handing OTP)
  • 4. Review period: 24 hours on business days
  • 5. Refund period upon approval: 7 business days

5. Financial Refund Mechanism

  • Amount returned to buyer's bank card via the same secure payment gateway used for the original payment
  • Through the original payment channel used
  • Appearance in account: 7-14 business days depending on the bank
  • Hasaadi does not bear refund fees imposed by the gateway or banks

  • WhatsApp: +968 9526 8707
  • Working hours: Sunday - Thursday, 9 AM - 6 PM
